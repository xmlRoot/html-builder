Throughout its history, the Old Republic faced numerous galactic conflicts, including the infamous Sith Wars, the Mandalorian Wars, and the Jedi Civil War. These wars were pivotal in shaping the galaxy's political and social landscape. The Sith Empire, after a long absence, returned from deep space, launching a series of attacks that tested the Republic's resilience. The Jedi, often at the forefront of these battles, played a crucial role in defending the Republic, although not without significant losses and internal strife.
